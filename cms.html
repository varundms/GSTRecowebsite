Sync / Export

Posts are stored in your browser's local storage. Use the tools below to share posts with your intern, back them up, or move them to another device. Both you and your intern can manage posts — just share the exported data file.

📤 Export All Posts

Download all your blog posts as a JSON file. Share this with your intern so they can import it and manage posts from their browser.

📥 Import Posts

Import posts from a JSON file previously exported from this CMS. This will merge with existing posts (won't delete current posts).

🔄 How to sync with your intern

Since this is a local file-based website (no server), here's the recommended workflow:

  1. You or intern makes changes in the CMS
  2. Export posts using the button above
  3. Share the posts.json file via WhatsApp/email/Drive
  4. Other person clicks Import to load the latest posts
  5. The blog page auto-reads from the same storage

⚠️ Clear All Posts

Permanently delete all posts from this browser. This cannot be undone. Export first if you want a backup.
