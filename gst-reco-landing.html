Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI Matching work?

Our AI uses advanced algorithms to match entries across Books, GSTR-1, GSTR-2A, GSTR-2B, and GSTR-3B using name similarity, amount matching, and date triangulation. The system supports both Purchase and Sales reconciliation and learns from your data patterns to improve accuracy over time.

Can I use GST Reco for multiple companies?

Absolutely! Our multi-tenant architecture supports multiple organizations. You can manage reconciliations for multiple entities, business units, or clients from a single account with proper access controls.

What file formats are supported?

You can pull GSTR-1, GSTR-2A, GSTR-2B, and GSTR-3B directly from the GST portal, or upload Excel files (.xlsx, .xls) manually. Our intelligent system automatically detects and maps columns, making the process seamless. We support both Purchase and Sales reconciliation.

Is there a user limit to any of the subscription plans?

No, there is absolutely no limit to no. of users. You can have unlimited users on GST Reco.

What happens if entries don't match?

Unmatched entries are clearly categorized (In Books Only, In Portal Only, Mismatch, etc.) with detailed breakdowns. You can use our AI-powered matching feature to resolve complex cases, or export the data for manual review.

Is there a client assignment feature for service providers/CAs?

Yes, you can assign clients to your company users in GST Reco.
